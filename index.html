characters

Zandros

Zandros is a mischievous tiefling with dark, red skin and a charming southern accent. She works for a mysterious organization somehow connected with the Zhentarim. Her pet crow, stilts, greets every guest with a shrill cry. She just wants to make a buck from the good folks of Saltmarsh and she longs for a machine that will let her explore the depths of the Sea of Swords. If she is anything, she's pernicious and suspicious.

Kreb Shankwit

Kreb is a bespectacled gnome with a carefully-waxed mustache and a variety of sharp-looking vests. He owns a lovely tavern called The Empty Net on the edge of Saltmarsh. Known to draw a shadier crowd, his patrons know to behave themselves while on the premises. Not fond of the Lord's Alliance, your secrets are safe with him.

Gellan Primewater

Primewater is a youthful-looking man, a member of the Saltmarsh town council, and an 'entrepeneur'. His merchant fleets move spirits and expensive cloth up and down the Sword Coast. He's tall, fond of fine crimson livery, and knows how to throw a good party.
